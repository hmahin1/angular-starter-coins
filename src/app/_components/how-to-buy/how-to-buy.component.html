6. List your player on the market first: Buy Now Price: ≤60% of the Max Price(if max price ＞10K), Duration: 1day.

7. Input your player’s correct info, then click “Search”. Select your player card from the list, and then click “Submit”.




8. You can check the coins transfer process from the “Delivery History”.
